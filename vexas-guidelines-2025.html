VEXAS Overview

  • Vacuoles, E1 enzyme, X-linked, Autoinflammatory, Somatic
  • Caused by somatic mutations in UBA1 gene
  • Affects males >50 years (rare in females)
  • Inflammatory + Hematologic manifestations
  • High morbidity and mortality
